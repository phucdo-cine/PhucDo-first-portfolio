NOCTURNE LUMINOUS
We are organizing this event to create a positive and nurturing space for young people through a healing music exhibition. Imagine yourself on an island, taking a nighttime stroll and enjoying the gentle sounds around you. In this exhibition, you not only witness the wonders that only appear at night, but also get to create your own unique melodies. By doing so, they can release negative feelings and share a sense of peace and take their time to admire the nature. Music, after all, is more than just sound. It is a powerful language that allows artists to convey their thoughts, stories, and emotional journeys to the world.
