ONCE UPON A TIME

Since ancient times, Vietnamese folk tales have conveyed cultural traditions and moral values ​​passed down through generations. This project presents twelve visual icons illustrating famous stories through a modern design perspective. The artwork retains soft lines to evoke a sense of warmth and familiarity, ideal for children's stories. The color palette is based on analogous schemes and complementary separation. By combining traditional folklore with modern aesthetics, these timeless stories become more appealing and accessible to all audiences.
