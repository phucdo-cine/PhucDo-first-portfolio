HOW TO BE CONFIDENT ON

YOUR FIRST DATE
In this modern day, there are variety reasons for a girl to be confident. Among them, we believe that love yourself should be the foremost priority to become confident and beautiful. Therefore, we create this short story inspiring from the legendary fairytale “The little Mermaid”. We wish for every girls from all age out there to take care of themselves, love themselves and be more confident. First impressions have always been important in many situation to relationships around us. So we would like to encourage and show people simple 8 steps on preparing for important outings, in the case of our story, a first date, so that they can enjoy their moment to the fullest.
